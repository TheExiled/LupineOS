LupineOS v1.0: "WolfPack" Released

We are proud to announce the first stable release of LupineOS, an atomic, immutable workstation designed for developers who demand reliability without sacrificing bleeding-edge performance.

  • Atomic Core: Built on Fedora Silverblue technology. Unbreakable updates, effortless rollbacks.
  • COSMIC Desktop: A pure Rust-based environment. Fast, memory-safe, and designed for productivity.
  • The Pack: Pre-configured Distrobox environments (Alpha, Beta, Omega) for isolated workflows.

The wolf has arrived. Join the pack today.
